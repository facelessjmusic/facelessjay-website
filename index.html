Faceless Jay is an independent masked music artist from Traverse City, Michigan releasing originals and covers. Official website for music releases, biography, and contact information.

In late 2023, a machine learning large language model ripped straight from open-source Chinese software unfortunately gained sentience after being fed too much Frank Zappa and Janelle Monáe.

Its creator, a software engineer who wishes to remain anonymous, followed in the path of Icarus and gave the hyper sentient AI a body — though “the facial expressions looked like a mannequin who listens to Primus.”

Out of shame and a desire to blow up on TikTok, this once nameless abomination donned a mask and took on a name:

Faceless Jay
